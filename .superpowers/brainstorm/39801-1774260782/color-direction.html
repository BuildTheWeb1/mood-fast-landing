Color & Mood Direction

The Juno design uses soft lavender/purple gradients. How should we adapt that for MoodFast?

A — Keep current palette

Blue (#60B5FF) + mint (#83F6CC) + dark (#1A1A1A). Apply Juno's soft gradient mesh background and layout style but stay with MoodFast's existing brand colors.

B — Shift toward lavender/purple

Closer to Juno's palette. Swap the blue for a warmer indigo/lavender, keep the mint accent. Feels more calming and wellness-oriented.

C — Warm sunset palette

Peach, rose, and soft lavender. Evokes emotional warmth and approachability — a softer wellness feel, different from the current cool blues.
