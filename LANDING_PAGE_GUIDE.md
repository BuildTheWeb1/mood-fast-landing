- Asset: `AppLogo` (available in Assets.xcassets)

### FAQ Section

**Q: Is Mood Fast free?**  
A: Yes! Mood Fast is free to download and use. The free version includes mood tracking, basic insights, and access to 10 wellness activities. PRO unlocks unlimited features.

**Q: How long does it take to log a mood?**  
A: Just 2 seconds! Simply tap how you're feeling and you're done. Add optional notes if you want to journal more.

**Q: Is my data private?**  
A: Absolutely. Your mood data is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell your personal information.

**Q: Can I export my data?**  
A: PRO users can export their mood history as CSV, JSON, or PDF reports at any time.

**Q: Does it work offline?**  
A: Yes! You can log moods offline and they'll sync when you're back online.
